FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free physical inspections?

Yes. S&I Roofing Ltd provides 100% free physical roof assessments. We inspect your slates, flashing, chimney stack, valleys, or gutters, and supply a written, itemised quote. There are no call-out fees or obligations.

What roof repairs do you specialize in?

We handle all roof-related repairs. This includes replacing broken slate or tiles, leak detection, chimney lead apron re-leadings, valley upgrades, Velux skylight reseals, guttering cracks, and dry verge wind lock upgrades.

Are you licensed and fully insured?

Yes, S&I Roofing Ltd is a registered roofing business. We carry complete public liability and employer liability insurance coverage, providing full peace of mind for residential and commercial works anywhere in Ireland.

Do you offer a warranty on your roofing works?

Yes. All our roofing projects come with a written warranty guarantee. We offer 5 years on minor tiles/flashing leak repairs, and up to 25 years on new slate, insulation, lead valley, or gutter installations.
